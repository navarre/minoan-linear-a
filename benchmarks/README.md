# Benchmarks

Benchmarks are structured tests that use the corpus and project data to recover known results, test new claims, or disprove weak hypotheses.

## Categories
- **replication** — re-discover known results from corpus structure
- **exploratory** — detect patterns worth further study
- **disproof** — test whether a published or proposed claim fails against the corpus

## Status labels
- **draft** — initial write-up
- **provisional success** — meaningful result, but still limited or heuristic
- **inconclusive** — signal too weak or method too limited
- **failed** — benchmark did not recover the target result
