4. Sign Inventory Discrepancies

Our sign inventory (signs.py) has 106 syllabograms. The corpus contains 356 unique sign types. There is a large gap.

106In signs.py
356In Corpus
275In Corpus, NOT in Inventory
25In Inventory, NOT in Corpus
Explanation: Our signs.py only covers syllabograms (106 entries). The corpus contains 356 sign types including logograms (A500-A662 series), fractions (A701-A717), complex signs, ligatures, and variant forms. The 275 "missing" signs are mostly logograms and complex signs that were never added to signs.py. The 25 inventory signs not in the corpus may be extremely rare or only attested in documents not yet in SigLA.
