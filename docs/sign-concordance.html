Sign Concordance

Every attested occurrence of every Linear A sign, indexed from GORILA Volume 5

What this is. A complete transcription of the sign-by-sign attestation index from GORILA Volume 5 (Godart & Olivier, 1985, pp. 142–325). Every row below is one row from the printed index: one occurrence of one sign in one document, with its immediate sign-context and its source reference.

What this is not. This is not a word list and not a lexicon. The unit here is the sign attestation, not the lexeme. Linear A has no agreed word count; any page claiming to list "every word in Minoan" is overclaiming. This concordance lets you see exactly where in the published corpus each sign appears, with what neighbours, and what surrounding context.

Scope. Loading… Covers Volume 5 printed pages 142–325. Fraction signs (A 701–A 713) and punctuation marks (page 325) are included.

Rubriques (the printed index's section codes): A alone · B bracketed · C broken · D as first sign · E as non-initial · F headers (PHAISTOS / Chania) · G as last sign · H composite catalog · J composite variants. Target-sign position is marked *like this* in the context column.
