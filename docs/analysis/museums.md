# Where to See Linear A

## Museums with Linear A Artifacts Worldwide

### Crete

**Heraklion Archaeological Museum** — The world's primary repository
- ~147 clay tablets from Haghia Triada (largest single archive)
- Tablets from Phaistos, Knossos, Tylissos, Malia, Gournia
- Gold ring with spiral Linear A inscription (HM 530, Room V)
- Fourteen-sided prism seal with Arkhanes script (Room VII)
- Large pithos with "su-ki-ri-te-i-ja" inscription
- Stone libation tables with the libation formula
- The Phaistos Disc (Room III)
- The Anetaki ivory scepter (~119 signs, recovered in 2017 excavations, published 2025)
- Online catalog: ca.heraklionmuseum.gr

**Chania Archaeological Museum** — Second largest LA collection
- ~99 Linear A tablets/documents from ancient Kydonia
- Roundels and sealings
- New building opened April 2022, 15 Skra Str., Chalepa

**Siteia Archaeological Museum** — Eastern Crete
- 31 Linear A tablets from the Palace of Kato Zakros (Showcase 16)
- Tablet fragments from Petras
- Nodules/sealings

**Agios Nikolaos Archaeological Museum** — Eastern Crete
- Fragments from Petras and Zakros
- "Goddess of Myrtos" statuette
- Reopened 2024 after renovation

**Rethymno Archaeological Museum**
- Clay sealings with Linear A from Archanes (15th c. BCE)

### Mainland Greece

**Athens National Archaeological Museum**
- Pithoi with Linear A from Thera (Santorini)
- Vase sherds from Akrotiri with Linear A signs

**Museum of Prehistoric Thera** (Fira, Santorini)
- 3 Linear A tablet fragments from Akrotiri
- 4 vase sherds with Linear A signs

### United Kingdom

**Ashmolean Museum, Oxford** — Largest LA collection outside Greece
- Evans's Knossos excavation archive (papers, notebooks, photos)
- World's largest collection of Aegean seals outside Greece
- Sealstones with Linear A and Cretan Hieroglyphic signs
- Online: collections.ashmolean.org, sirarthurevans.ashmus.ox.ac.uk

**British Museum, London** — Room 12a
- Bronze double-axe with two Linear A signs (G_1954-1020-1)
- Pottery and bronzes from Cretan tombs

### United States

**Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York**
- Clay tablets with Linear A
- Minoan collection in Greek and Roman Art dept
- Fully open-access online: metmuseum.org/art/collection

### France

**Musée du Louvre, Paris**
- Minoan seals and sealstones with Linear A signs
- Dept of Greek, Etruscan and Roman Antiquities

### Italy

**National Archaeological Museum, Florence**
- Linear A sealings from Italian excavations at Phaistos and Haghia Triada
- Often overlooked repository for early expedition finds
